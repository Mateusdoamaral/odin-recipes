Cabonara

Description:

Carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish that hails from Rome. It's known for its rich, creamy textureâ€”achieved without using cream.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon
  • 2 large egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps:

  • Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  • Cook guanciale in a pan until crispy.
  • Mix eggs, cheese & pepper in a bowl to make the sauce.
  • Drain pasta (reserve a bit of water), then mix it with guanciale off the heat.
  • Stir in egg mixture quickly, using a splash of pasta water to make it silky.
